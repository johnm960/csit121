Philadelphia Turkey and Cheese Croissant with Pineapple Slices

"I may not be good at cooking but this makes me feel some way"

Stuff you will need

  • Philadelphia Cream Cheese (Does not have to be this specific brand, any cream cheese will do)
  • croissant
  • Sliced Ham
  • AMERICAN Cheese
  • Butter (Optional)
  • Pineapple Slices (This is not optional)

How to make it

  1. Place down the croissant.
  2. Apply a thin spread of butter over the roof of the croissant.
  3. Split the croissant in two by slicing it horizontally.
  4. For the following steps, you will be operating on the sides where the croissant slices were initially bounded by.
  5. Apply a layer of cream cheese on one or both sides of the croissant.
  6. Add however many slices of cheese, sliced ham, cream cheese, or even nothing at all onto the sides of the croissant.
  7. Add the Pineapple Slices.
  8. Toast the croissant in a toaster oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 6-8 minutes or until its soft and flakey with the ingredients edible.
  9. After it's cooked, wait until its cool enough to retrieve and enjoy.
